Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-18

1. Data we collect

We collect account, profile, trip, booking, and payment-related metadata needed to operate the service, plus technical logs for security and reliability.

Data is used for authentication, bookings, notifications, support, fraud prevention, and platform operations.

Data is shared only with necessary service providers (for example payment and infrastructure providers) and as required by law.

We apply access controls, encryption-in-transit, and operational safeguards. Data is retained only for as long as required for service operation, legal obligations, and dispute handling.

5. User rights

Where applicable, users may request data access, correction, or deletion subject to legal constraints.

6. Contact

For privacy requests, contact support through the project support channel listed in the repository.
