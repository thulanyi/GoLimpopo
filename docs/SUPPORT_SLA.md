# Support and SLA

## Contact channels
- Primary support email: support@magayisa.example
- Secondary escalation: ops@magayisa.example

Replace the placeholder emails with your live support addresses before launch.

## Support hours
- Beta: Monday to Saturday, 08:00-20:00 SAST
- Critical incident escalation: 24/7

## Response targets
- Sev 1 (critical outage): first response within 15 minutes
- Sev 2 (major functionality degraded): first response within 1 hour
- Sev 3 (minor issue): first response within 1 business day

## Resolution targets
- Sev 1: mitigation within 1 hour, full resolution target within 4 hours
- Sev 2: mitigation within 4 hours, full resolution target within 1 business day
- Sev 3: resolution target within 5 business days

## User-facing communication
- For Sev 1 and Sev 2 incidents, publish updates at agreed cadence in the incident channel.
- Include impact scope, temporary workarounds, and expected next update time.
